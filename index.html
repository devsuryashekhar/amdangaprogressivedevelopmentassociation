About Us

Amdanga Progressive Development Association is a non-governmental social welfare group founded in 2025, committed to the welfare and development of communities in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India.

Our mission focuses on improving education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and social empowerment. We actively organize literacy programs, free health camps, skill development workshops for women, and environmental conservation initiatives. With a dedicated team of 10 members, we believe in fostering equality, equity, and community participation. Through volunteer work, local collaborations, and consistent outreach programs, we aim to create a society where every citizen has access to basic rights, education, and opportunities to thrive. Join us in our journey to make Kharagpur a model town for civic responsibility and social welfare.
